Connect Four Game

Instructions:

  • Player 1 is Red, Player 2 is Yellow.
  • Click on a column to drop your disc.
  • Take turns with your opponent.
  • First to align four discs in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins!
  • Click 'Reset Game' to start a new round.
  • Enjoy the game!
